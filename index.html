About Me

Internal Medicine Specialist

I am a highly qualified Internal Medicine Specialist with over 5 years of invaluable experience. Based in Nairobi, Kenya, I am fluent in English, Kiswahili, and Ekegusii, ensuring effective communication with my patients.

With my extensive background in internal medicine, I specialize in diagnosing, treating, and managing complex adult diseases, including chronic conditions like diabetes, hypertension, respiratory issues, and gastrointestinal disorders. My goal is to offer comprehensive care tailored to each patient's needs, ensuring a holistic approach to health and wellness.
